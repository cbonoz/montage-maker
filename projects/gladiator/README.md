# Gladiator — Montages

A collection of montages from Ridley Scott's epic drama about vengeance, honor, and the Roman Colosseum.

## Montages

### 1. gladiator_vengeance — "I will have my vengeance…"

**Status:** ✅ Built

**Hook (monologue):**
> "My name is Maximus Decimus Meridius, commander of the Armies of the North, General of the Felix Legions, loyal servant to the true emperor, Marcus Aurelius. Father to a murdered son, husband to a murdered wife. And I will have my vengeance, in this life or the next."

**Alternative hook (shorter version):**
> "I will have my vengeance, in this life or the next."

**Music:** Moonlight @ 75 BPM

**Output:** `output/gladiator_montage.mp4`

**Source clips used:**
- `hook_maximus_reveal.mp4` — Maximus removes his helmet in the Colosseum
- `scenes_compilation.mp4` — Gladiator best scenes compilation

**Scenes sampled:**
- Opening battle in Germania (establishing power and loss)
- Maximus in the fields and family home (tragedy setup)
- Commodus's arrival and betrayal
- Colosseum roaring crowds (scale and spectacle)
- Tiger fight sequences (raw action)
- Rome walking through the gates (triumph)
- Final battle and climax sequences

**Mood:** Vengeance, honor, gladiatorial epic, tragic hero

**Build Command:**

```bash
# Rebuild gladiator_vengeance montage
python build_montage.py projects/gladiator sounds/Moonlight.mp3 --bpm 75
```

## Notes

- The montage cuts between Maximus's intimate loss and the grand spectacle of the Colosseum
- Moonlight (75 BPM) provides nostalgic, epic pacing that respects both the personal and the grand
- The hook's full version (helmet reveal + monologue) is iconic and carries weight through the entire montage
- Best cut with long, powerful shots interspersed with quick crowd reaction cuts
